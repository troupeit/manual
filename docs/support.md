# Support

TroupeIT has a built-in support system to help you with any issues.

## Getting Help

### In-App Support

1. Click **Support** in the navigation menu
2. Fill out the support form with your question or issue
3. You'll receive an email confirmation with your ticket number

### Email Support

You can also email **support@troupeit.com** directly. Your email will create a support ticket automatically.

## Replying to Tickets

- Reply to support emails to add information to your ticket
- Use the secure reply address provided in notification emails
- Include your ticket number for faster service

## Support for Guests

Non-registered users can also submit support requests through the public support form on the TroupeIT website.

## Priority Support

Paid subscription plans include priority support with faster response times.
