# Subscription Plans

TroupeIT offers different subscription levels for companies.

## Free Plan

The free plan includes:

- 2 crew members
- 10 performers
- 1 event per month
- 2 shows per event
- Basic features

## Paid Plans

Paid plans are available as Monthly, 6-Month, or Annual subscriptions and include:

- Unlimited crew members
- Unlimited performers
- Unlimited events
- Unlimited shows
- PDF exports
- API access
- Priority support

## Managing Your Subscription

1. Go to the company page
2. Click **Billing** (requires Owner or Producer access)
3. View current plan and usage
4. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel as needed

## Payment Processing

Payment is processed securely through Stripe. From the Billing page you can:

- Update your payment method
- View billing history
- Download invoices
- Manage subscription renewal
