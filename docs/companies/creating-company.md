# Creating a Company

## Steps to Create a Company

1. From your Dashboard, click **New Company**
2. Enter the company name and description
3. Configure company settings (see [Company Settings](/docs/companies/company-settings))
4. Click **Create Company**

As the creator, you automatically become the company **Owner** with full access.

## What's Next?

After creating your company, you'll want to:

1. [Configure your company settings](/docs/companies/company-settings)
2. [Invite members](/docs/companies/inviting-members) to join
3. [Create your first event](/docs/events/creating-events)
