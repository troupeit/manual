# Company Settings

Configure your company to match your production needs.

## Company Information

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Name** | Your company or production name |
| **Description** | A brief description shown to potential members |
| **Long Description** | Detailed information about your company |
| **Avatar** | Company logo or image |
| **Cover Image** | Banner image for the company page |

## Privacy Settings

Control who can see and join your company:

- **Private Listings**: Hide your company from public searches
- **Require Invites**: Members must be invited to join (vs. open membership)

## Invitation Settings

Control who can invite new members:

- **Admins only**: Only Producers and Owners can invite new members
- **Admins and existing members**: Any member can share the invitation link
