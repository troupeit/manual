# Adding Acts and Notes

Build your show by adding acts and notes to the schedule.

## Adding Submitted Acts

1. Open the show editor
2. Find the act in the **Submissions** panel
3. Click the act to add it to the end of the schedule
4. Alternatively, use **Add Act** to search for any act in your company

## Adding Notes

Notes are non-performance items like intermissions or DJ sets.

1. Click **Add Note**
2. Enter the note text (e.g., "Intermission", "DJ Set - House Music")
3. Set the duration
4. Choose a color for visual distinction
5. Click **Add**

## Note Colors

Use different colors to quickly identify note types:
- Blue for intermissions
- Green for DJ/music sets
- Yellow for announcements
- Red for important notices

:::tip
Consistent color coding helps your crew quickly scan the cue sheet.
:::
