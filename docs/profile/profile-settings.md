# Profile Settings

Access your profile settings by clicking **Settings** in the navigation menu or the edit button on your dashboard profile card.

## Profile Information

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Display Name** | How you appear to other users |
| **Username** | Your unique @username for mentions and your profile URL |
| **Email** | Your login email (can be changed) |
| **Phone Number** | Optional, for SMS notifications |
| **Avatar** | Your profile picture |
| **Cover Image** | Banner image for your profile page |

## Privacy Settings

You can control what information is shared with other company members:

- Choose whether to share your email with company members
- Choose whether to share your phone number with company members
