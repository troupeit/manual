# Creating an Account

Anyone can create a free account on TroupeIT and start a **30-day free trial** with full access to all features.

## Sign Up Process

1. Visit [troupeit.com](https://troupeit.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Complete the security verification (this helps prevent spam accounts)
3. Enter your email address and choose a secure password
4. Fill in your display name and username
5. Click **Create Account**

## Your Free Trial

When you create your account, you automatically get a **30-day free trial**. During this time:

- ✅ **Full access** to all features (unlimited events, shows, crew, etc.)
- ✅ Create your first company at no cost
- ✅ Explore everything TroupeIT has to offer

After your trial ends, you can [subscribe to a paid plan](/docs/companies/subscription-plans) to continue with unlimited access.

## Password Security Tips

- Use a unique password that you don't use on other sites
- Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Consider using a password manager

After signing up, you'll be guided through a brief onboarding wizard to set up your profile and create your first company.
