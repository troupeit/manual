# Helpful Tips

Best practices for using TroupeIT effectively.

## Before Your Event

1. **Verify membership**: Make sure all performers have joined your company and submitted their acts before the deadline

2. **Review submissions**: Check that all acts have complete information and media files

3. **Build the schedule**: Create your show schedule and share it with performers

4. **Test the flow**: Review the cue sheet for timing and transitions

## Day of Show

1. **Download assets early**: Get all media files and test with your playback system

2. **Brief your crew**: Make sure everyone knows how to use Live View

3. **Test connectivity**: Verify internet access works at the venue

4. **Print backups**: Have paper copies of the cue sheet ready

## During the Show

1. **Keep Live View open**: Multiple devices provide redundancy

2. **Use chat**: Communicate with your team through the built-in chat

3. **Stay connected**: Keep devices charged and connected

4. **Follow the stage manager**: One person should control cue advancement

## After the Show

1. **Export records**: Generate a PDF of the final cue sheet for your records

2. **Review timing**: Note what worked and what to adjust for next time

3. **Thank your team**: Celebrate a successful show!
